Privacy Policy

Bible Trace Map

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

Bible Trace Map ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, then do not use our App.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Preferences: Language selection, map style preferences, theme settings
  • Bookmarks: Content you save or mark as favorites
  • Feedback: Any bug reports or feedback you submit

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, screen resolution
  • Usage Analytics: Which features you use, time spent in app (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: Error logs to help us fix issues (non-personal data)
  • Location: Optional location data (only when you explicitly enable maps location feature)

C. Map Tile Data

  • Map Tiles: Downloaded from OpenStreetMap (third-party service)
  • We do not control or store the map tile providers' data collection practices

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • App Functionality: To provide core app features and services
  • Improvements: To fix bugs, improve performance, and enhance features
  • Support: To respond to your support requests
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app (anonymized and aggregated)
  • Personalization: To customize your experience based on preferences you select

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT:

  • Sell, rent, or lease your personal information
  • Share personal information with third parties for marketing
  • Disclose identifiable personal information without consent

We MAY Share:

  • Aggregated Data: Non-personally identifiable, aggregated analytics
  • Service Providers: With vendors strictly necessary for app operation (map services)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

OpenStreetMap

  • Provides map tiles and satellite imagery
  • OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy
  • Map tiles are cached locally; we don't control their data practices

Analytics Providers (if enabled)

  • Firebase Analytics (optional, Google's privacy policy applies)
  • Fully anonymized event tracking

5. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All event data is stored locally on your device
  • Your preferences and bookmarks are stored in app's private storage
  • Only accessible to the app (not to other apps unless you grant permission)

Security Measures

  • We use industry-standard security practices
  • Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS)
  • No sensitive personal data is transmitted to our servers

Data Retention

  • Settings and preferences: Stored until you uninstall the app
  • Analytics: Retained for up to 12 months in aggregated form
  • Crash reports: Deleted after 30 days

6. Your Privacy Rights

Access and Correction

  • Contact us to request what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information

Deletion

  • You can clear all app data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data (backups may vary by device)

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale/sharing of personal information

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If located in the EU:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object

7. Children's Privacy

Bible Trace Map does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). If we become aware we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Location Information

How We Use Location

  • Maps: Display your location on interactive maps (optional feature)
  • Analytics: May anonymously track app usage by region (aggregated)

Location Permissions

  • Location is only accessed when you explicitly enable the "Show my location" feature
  • You can revoke location permission at any time through device settings

Precise Location

  • We do NOT collect precise GPS coordinates without explicit user permission
  • Approximate location based on network connectivity is non-identifiable

9. Advertising and Marketing

Bible Trace Map contains no advertisements and does not use ad networks. We respect your experience.

We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
