Welcome to BibleTrace Map (also referred to as BibleChronos). We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing an interactive experience in exploring biblical and world history.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We believe in a private user experience. Therefore:

  • We do not require user registration or accounts.
  • We do not collect personal identification information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • We do not access your contacts or precise GPS location.

2. Information We Collect (Analytics)

To improve app performance and user experience, we use Google Firebase Analytics. This service collects anonymous technical data, such as:

  • Device model and operating system version.
  • General geographic location (City/Country level).
  • App usage statistics (e.g., which timeline or journey is most viewed).

3. Data Storage (Capacitor & LocalStorage)

Since the app is built using Capacitor/Web technology, we use LocalStorage on your device to save your preferences, such as:

  • Theme settings (Light/Dark mode).
  • Language preference (English/Telugu).
  • Measurement units (KM/Miles).

This data stays on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app stability.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used for usage statistics.
  • OpenStreetMap/Leaflet: Used for rendering historical maps.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our app provides educational content suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
